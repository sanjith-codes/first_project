Seven Wonders of the World

Great Wall of China

  • Over 21,000 km long
  • Built to protect China from invasions
  • Constructed over many dynasties

Petra (Jordan)

  • Ancient city carved in rock
  • Known as the "Rose City"
  • Built around 300 BC

Christ the Redeemer (Brazil)

  • Statue in Rio de Janeiro
  • 38 meters tall
  • Symbol of Christianity

Machu Picchu (Peru)

  • Incan city in the Andes
  • Built in the 15th century
  • Hidden for centuries

Chichen Itza (Mexico)

  • Mayan pyramid (El Castillo)
  • Used for astronomy
  • Famous for shadow effect

Roman Colosseum (Italy)

  • Ancient amphitheater
  • Held 50,000 spectators
  • Used for gladiator fights

Taj Mahal (India)

  • Built by Shah Jahan
  • Symbol of love
  • Made of white marble
